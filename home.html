About us

The Sparks Foundation Bank (TSF) a Fortune 500 company, is a Multinational, Public Sector Banking and Financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai. The rich heritage and legacy of over 200 years, accredits TSF as the most trusted Bank by people through generations. TSF, the largest Bank with 1/4th market share, serves over 44 crore customers through its vast network of over 22,000 branches, 58,500 ATMs, 66,000 BC outlets, with an undeterred focus on innovation, and customer centricity, which stems from the core values of the Bank - Service, Transparency, Ethics, Politeness and Sustainability.

The Bank has successfully diversified .... businesses through its 11 subsidiaries i.e TSF General Insurance, TSF Life Insurance, TSF Mutual Fund, TSF Card, etc. It has spread its presence globally and operates across time zones through 233 offices in 32 foreign countries. Growing with times, TSF continues to redefine banking in India, as it aims to offer responsible and sustainable Banking solutions.
